Welcome to Golden Jackets Brazil!

The Golden Jackets Brazil community celebrates cloud leaders who are passionate about learning and committed to helping others succeed. Our members have achieved all active AWS certifications and actively support others on their cloud journey.

Already a Golden Jacket holder from Brazil? Get in touch to join the community and be featured on this page.

About Us

We are the Golden Jackets from Brazil — a select group of professionals recognized by Amazon Web Services with the highest distinction in the AWS community worldwide. The Golden Jacket is awarded to those who have earned all active AWS certifications and demonstrated exceptional contribution through technical leadership, content creation, community building, and innovation. Our mission is to share knowledge, inspire the next generation of cloud leaders, and strengthen Brazil's presence on the global AWS stage.

This is an independent community, not officially affiliated with Amazon Web Services.
